# Apéry数

$$
\begin{aligned}
A_n&=\sum_{k=0}^n\binom nk^2\binom{n+k}k^2=\sum_{k=0}^n\frac{[{(n+k)!}]^2}{(k!)^4[{(n-k)!}]^2},\\
B_n&=\sum_{k=0}^n\binom nk^2\binom{n+k}k=\sum_{k=0}^n\frac{(n+k)!}{(k!)^3[{(n-k)!}]^2},\\
\end{aligned}
$$
